3. Third-Party Services We Use

Brillora uses the following verified services:

No Facebook Audience Network, no Applovin, no external ad mediation is used.

4. Location Permission

We request location access only to suggest nearby tutors. You can deny this permission, and the app will still work with limited features. We do not track your live movement or collect background location data.

5. Advertisements

Brillora uses Google AdMob for showing ads. Ads may collect general device identifiers for reporting purposes. No sensitive personal information is sold or shared with advertisers.

6. Data Protection & Security

Your data is stored securely through Firebase servers. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third-party company.
