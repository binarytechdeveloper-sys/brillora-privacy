Brillora - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 December, 2025

Developer: Brillora / Soumit Chatterjee

1. Introduction

Brillora is a tutor-student learning application designed to support class scheduling, communication, and educational activities. By using Brillora, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our data handling practices.

2. Information We Collect

A) Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User ID (Firebase Authentication)

B) User Data & Content

  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Chat messages & class-related content
  • Uploaded files or documents (optional)

C) Location Data

  • Approximate location for better tutor matching

D) App Activity & Logs

  • Crash reports & diagnostics
  • App performance analytics
  • Device or app identifiers

E) Payment Information

Payments are processed through third-party UPI apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and Razorpay. We DO NOT store banking details, UPI PIN, debit/credit card information, or passwords.

3. How We Use Data

  • To manage accounts and login
  • To schedule and manage classes
  • To enable chat and communication
  • To send notifications and class alerts
  • To improve app performance & stability

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may be shared only for essential services:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore Database
  • UPI payment providers when payments are initiated by users

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit. Firebase securely stores essential data. We follow standard security practices but cannot guarantee 100% security due to internet limitations.
